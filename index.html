WELCOME

Conveniently located at Red Hill Shopping Center, The Hub is the happening Marin burger spot where locals go to dine, drink and socialize. While our grassfed burgers are divine, our menu also features healthy salads with local produce, vegetarian and vegan options, and the best crispy chicken sandwiches in Marin. We strive to connect people to each other and to their community by hosting Dine & Donate events throughout the year. Owned and operated by a San Anselmo family, we are looking forward to welcoming you to have a great dining experience at The Hub.

ABOUT US

The Hub is a casual dining spot operated by a family committed to serving cravable food that is sourced from nearby produce farms, grass-fed beef ranches, and craft breweries. Many names were being considered based on our menu of grass-fed, grass-finished burgers and farm fresh salads...Farmstead, Sorich Ranch, Drake’s Farm...but in reality, we are located at Red Hill Shopping Center near the baseball fields, tennis courts and playground at Memorial Park, the turf soccer fields at Red Hill Community Park, and walking distance from Archie Williams High School...a literal "hub" of activity.

When we decided to name our cozy spot The Hub, the vision for embracing San Anselmo’s history allowed us to highlight the surrounding open space with picturesque Red Hill as our backdrop. The Hub is located on what was once a country dirt road, surrounded by ranches and dairy barns with a critical train depot at it's center.

Thank you to the San Anselmo Historical Society for allowing us to use black & white historical photos for our interior.
